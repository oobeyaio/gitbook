# Updating The License Key

**1. Click on the Administration icon from the sidebar**

#### 2. Click on the "Go to Admin Settings" button

<figure><img src="https://images.tango.us/public/screenshot_81ac801d-a36c-4008-ba2a-6068dc4068a4.png?fm=png&#x26;crop=focalpoint&#x26;fit=crop&#x26;fp-x=0.3497&#x26;fp-y=0.3497&#x26;fp-z=1.4295&#x26;w=1200&#x26;mark-w=0.2&#x26;mark-pad=0&#x26;mark64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL21hZGUtd2l0aC10YW5nby13YXRlcm1hcmsucG5n&#x26;ar=3584%3A2032" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### 3. Click on "License Management" to navigate to the license configuration page

<figure><img src="https://images.tango.us/public/edited_image_25f39847-93f6-4eee-89cf-2678fae2f3d2.png?fm=png&#x26;crop=focalpoint&#x26;fit=crop&#x26;fp-x=0.3025&#x26;fp-y=0.3025&#x26;fp-z=1.6525&#x26;w=1200&#x26;mark-w=0.2&#x26;mark-pad=0&#x26;mark64=aHR0cHM6Ly9pbWFnZXMudGFuZ28udXMvc3RhdGljL21hZGUtd2l0aC10YW5nby13YXRlcm1hcmsucG5n&#x26;ar=3584%3A2032" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### 4. You can renew your license key and see the license expiration date and limitations from this panel.
