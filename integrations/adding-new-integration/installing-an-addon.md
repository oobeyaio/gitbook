# Installing An Addon

## **OVERVIEW**

The addons placed in Oobeya Integrations allow you to **connect your SDLC/ALM tools with Oobeya** to enable _**real-time quality monitoring**_.

[Oobeya Integrations](https://oobeya.io/integrations/) is the main page where you browse and install your addons.

![](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MGIlBSTjQtZxUoFwUx4%2Fuploads%2FKApFdv3XgSlFf72Hhkie%2Ffile.png?alt=media)

## **ADDON CATEGORIES**

Addons are divided into six main categories:

* SCM & CI/CD
* Code Quality & Security
* Test & QA
* Monitoring/APM
* Project Management
* Extras

## **INSTALLING AN ADDON**

1. Navigate to **Oobeya Integrations**.
2. Click the addon you want to install.
3. Click the "**Install**" button.&#x20;

![](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MGIlBSTjQtZxUoFwUx4%2Fuploads%2FIZ4TU6AVDUeFK7BzjuNM%2Ffile.png?alt=media)
