# 🎁 Oobeya 2023 Q2 - Release Notes

&#x20;:tada: Exciting updates and improvements await you in Oobeya's Quarter 2 release! We've introduced new features and made enhancements to streamline your workflow and provide a better user experience.

## ​🎁 NEW FEATURES

1. **DORA Metrics for Trunk-Based Development Practices**
   * Oobeya now supports DORA Metrics tracking tailored for trunk-based development practices. Gain deeper insights into your development processes and enhance your team's performance.
2. **Ready-to-Use Oobeya Deployment API**
   * Seamlessly integrate Oobeya with your deployment processes using our newly launched Deployment API. Experience a smoother and more flexible deployment workflow to measure and track your DORA Metrics.
3. **Ready-to-Use Oobeya API**
   * Unlock the full potential of Oobeya with our new API. Easily integrate Oobeya with your existing tools and systems, empowering you to create bulk analysis on Oobeya and extend your reporting capabilities beyond the Oobeya platform.
4. **Symptom Root Cause Identification**
   * Identify the symptomatic units/areas to address issues with Oobeya's Symptoms module. We've enhanced the system to identify and present root causes of symptoms, enabling more effective issue resolution.
5. **Pull Request Revert Rate Metric**
   * Identify reverted pull requests and calculate the Pull Request Revert Rate metric. This valuable insight helps teams assess the effectiveness of their development processes and make data-driven improvements.
6. **DORA Metrics Support for Bitbucket Pipelines**
   * Start tracking your deployments running on Bitbucket Pipelines to measure DORA Metrics. Oobeya now supports Bitbucket Pipelines, providing a comprehensive view of your software development and delivery processes.

***

## :muscle: IMPROVEMENTS

* **Gitwiser Analysis Enhancements**
  * The analysis is now re-analyzed in the new reanalysis period if it failed or has a new commit. Additionally, auto-retry functionality for git, pull request, and deployment analysis has been introduced for a smoother workflow.
* **Performance Enhancements**
  * Experience improved performance across various aspects of Oobeya, including pull request analysis, DORA analysis, and symptom detection.
* **UI/UX Enhancements**
  * Navigate seamlessly to external tools, enjoy design enhancements, and benefit from new descriptions and tooltips. Our UI/UX improvements are designed to enhance your overall Oobeya experience.

We're committed to continuously improving Oobeya to meet your evolving needs. If you have any questions or feedback, our support team is here to assist you. Here's to a more efficient and productive software delivery journey with Oobeya!
