# Adding Notifications for Slack

## OVERVIEW&#x20;

Oobeya has an integration with Slack to send notifications and reports to your own chat channels. See the steps below to add channels and incoming webhooks for Slack.&#x20;

{% hint style="info" %}
After creating incoming webhooks, you can connect the Oobeya notification service with Slack.
{% endhint %}

## 1. CREATING A NEW TEAM CHANNEL ON SLACK&#x20;

1. Open Slack and click the "**Add channels**" icon.&#x20;

![](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MGIlBSTjQtZxUoFwUx4%2Fuploads%2FYDIDZrOSqJduKQLMnNgw%2Ffile.png?alt=media)

2\. Select the "Create a channel" option.&#x20;

![](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MGIlBSTjQtZxUoFwUx4%2Fuploads%2FeLsIfK6DMk4I2j50RVMh%2Ffile.png?alt=media)

3\.  Enter the channel name and select the privacy option. Then click the "**Create**" button.

![](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MGIlBSTjQtZxUoFwUx4%2Fuploads%2FaU7WlF9vcVQImgtkCyAq%2Ffile.png?alt=media)

4\. You can either add people to your channel or skip this step.&#x20;

![If you want to skip, click "Skip for Now".](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MGIlBSTjQtZxUoFwUx4%2Fuploads%2FGwpop0uSN9LaArcG21LZ%2Ffile.png?alt=media)

![If you prefer, you can add users to your channel.](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MGIlBSTjQtZxUoFwUx4%2Fuploads%2FzWElD2dsvgbyvWoT8xbv%2Ffile.png?alt=media)

5\. After all steps are done, it takes the final form as in the following.&#x20;

![](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MGIlBSTjQtZxUoFwUx4%2Fuploads%2FGGxBFbTxbBJt1Xw77Rqs%2Ffile.png?alt=media)

## 2. ADDING WEBHOOKS TO SLACK CHANNELS :electric\_plug:&#x20;

1. Click the **"Add an app"** link.&#x20;

![Option 1](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MGIlBSTjQtZxUoFwUx4%2Fuploads%2FVam0eah0F1QSAFsLywrp%2Ffile.png?alt=media)

![Option 2: Navigate to channel info and click "Add apps".](<../.gitbook/assets/image (120).png>)

2\. Click the "View / Add" button for "**Incoming Webhooks**".&#x20;

![](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MGIlBSTjQtZxUoFwUx4%2Fuploads%2FN3UzPmz2IUvZ0xSVFvam%2Ffile.png?alt=media)

3\. Click the "**Configuration**" button.&#x20;

![](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MGIlBSTjQtZxUoFwUx4%2Fuploads%2FUlOw8kqBPZHvqrGidIsK%2Ffile.png?alt=media)

4\. After you are navigated to the browser, click the "**Add To Slack**" button.

![](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MGIlBSTjQtZxUoFwUx4%2Fuploads%2F8JvNtluJfGAn8rOA8JOo%2Ffile.png?alt=media)

5\. Choose your channel or username, then click the "**Add Incoming WebHooks Integration**".&#x20;

![](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MGIlBSTjQtZxUoFwUx4%2Fuploads%2FtvaoGHVH5Qzy7KGhsSmP%2Ffile.png?alt=media)

6\. Now, you have added the Incoming Webhook URL. Copy this URL to the clipboard. (It is useful to keep it somewhere).

![](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MGIlBSTjQtZxUoFwUx4%2Fuploads%2FD83bJ5vrzlzsMdwS4TIH%2Ffile.png?alt=media)

7\. You can always manage your integration below the page;&#x20;

![](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MGIlBSTjQtZxUoFwUx4%2Fuploads%2FhOv5m1TMDRlwvSNqBpcO%2Ffile.png?alt=media)

## 3. ADDING NOTIFICATIONS ON OOBEYA :bell:&#x20;

1\. Open the **Profiles & Teams**. Select the _Profiles_ or _Teams_ tab to add a new notification.&#x20;

2\. Click the **Add Notification** button and configure the notification settings.&#x20;

![](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MGIlBSTjQtZxUoFwUx4%2Fuploads%2FaSGHZNdEV7htoXd2qBIg%2Ffile.png?alt=media)

3\. You can test the connection via the "**Send Notification Now**" button.&#x20;

4\. Click **Add** to save the notification settings.&#x20;

{% hint style="success" %}
That's it! Now you have a scheduled notifier for your Slack channel.
{% endhint %}
